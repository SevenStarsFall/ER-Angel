⚠️ Patient Frank – Pathways to Serious Risk

Review these high-risk scenarios before any intervention. Full details and avoidance strategies are in the Critical Pathways page.

  • Rapid ping-ponging between opioids → collapsed relief windows + escalated minimum dose
  • Unmonitored benzo + opioid overlap → respiratory depression
  • Inconsistent dosing intervals → micro-withdrawal cycles
  • Ignoring cross-tolerance when rotating agents

Always prioritize steady-state dosing and clear documentation.
